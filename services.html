We provide an extensive range of services from general practice, advanced surgery, cancer care, overnight hospitalisation through to grooming, kitten adoption, and dietary specific pet food.

There is nothing we don't professionally cater for.

Standard consultations are by appointment. Please contact us on 9497 3003 to book your pet in.

House calls are available by pre-arranged appointment only. This may be preferred in the event you are unable to travel or your pet suffers severe anxiety coming to the vet clinic.

Our normal hours of operation are:

Monday-Friday 8.00am to 6.00pm
Saturday 8.30am to 2.00pm
Sunday 10.00am to 12.00pm

For emergencies outside of these hours, please still call our main number 94973003, your call will be diverted to Dr. Rob's emergency phone. (This call is complimentary, however, if an after-hours appointment is arranged for you to meet Dr Rob at the clinic there is an additional after-hours fee).
